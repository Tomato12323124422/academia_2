PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)
Project Name: ACADEMIA LMS
1. Product Overview
1.1 Purpose

ACADEMIA LMS is a web-based Learning Management System designed to facilitate course delivery, assessment management, attendance tracking, and academic monitoring. The system supports live virtual classes via Zoom, QR-code-based attendance, and gamification to improve student engagement.

1.2 Problem Statement

Many institutions require a lightweight LMS that is easy to deploy, simple to use, and capable of running on free hosting platforms without requiring payment details.

1.3 Objectives

Deliver structured online learning.

Enable assignment submission and grading.

Allow guardians to monitor performance.

Support live virtual classes.

Track attendance using QR codes.

Increase engagement through gamification.

Deploy fully on free platforms.
